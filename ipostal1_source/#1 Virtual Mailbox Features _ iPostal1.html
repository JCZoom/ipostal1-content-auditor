The #1 digital mailbox service in the world

Digital Mailbox Features

  • U.S. or international mailing address

    Get a real street address with your personal or company mailbox number — not a PO box. Receive mail and packages from any carrier. Functionality for banking, credit cards, and personal or business use. Check local registration laws. Add address locations with just a click.

  • Digital mailbox

    Manage your mail and packages from anywhere, 24/7, with an app for Android or iPhone, or go online. See a picture of all the items in your inbox. With just a click, tell us what you want to do with each item - forward, open and scan, shred, recycle or pick it up.

  • Business phone and fax

    Virtual office plans include a Local or Toll-Free company phone number with personalized greeting, domestic and international phone forwarding, and digital voicemail. Dedicated electronic fax.

  • Document scanning

    When you receive mail that requires your immediate attention, request a scan and view a PDF of the mail content in your digital mailbox in hours. Store your mail and messages in the cloud - you get unlimited free cloud storage for scans and voice messages.

  • Mail and package forwarding

    Forward mail and packages anywhere in the U.S. See real time-shipping cost comparisons for different carriers and service levels. International forwarding is also available. You can even save up to 80% on international shipping from the U.S. Check out the Discounted International Forwarding Locations page.

  • Package consolidation

    Combine multiple packages into one box to save big on shipping. Combine letters into one package to save on forwarding.

  • Check deposit

    Let us deposit a check directly into your account, as long as your bank accepts mail-in deposits. Stop worrying about the security of checks at an unattended mailbox when you are away from home or the office, or abroad. Not available at all locations.

  • Shred or discard

    Choose to discard or shred unwanted documents. We shred on-site for the ultimate in security and privacy.

Some features have an additional cost. View pricing pages for details.
