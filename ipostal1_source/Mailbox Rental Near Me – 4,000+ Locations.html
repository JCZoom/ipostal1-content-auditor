How Much Does Mailbox Rental Cost?

Private mailbox rental costs vary, depending on the level of service you choose and your provider. While traditional mailbox rentals can be an option, they often lack the security, convenience, and features offered with online mailbox rental services. Here's a comparison of mailbox rental pricing and features for iPostal1, UPS Access Point Locker, and USPS PO Boxes to help you decide:

Traditional USPS PO Boxes vs. iPostal1 Virtual Mailboxes vs. UPS Access Point Lockers

1. Traditional PO Boxes:

  • Bargain Pricing: USPS PO Box rental prices start at $4.83 per month for the smallest size and can go up to $24.00 per month for the largest size.
  • Limited Features: Traditional USPS PO Boxes offer basic mail receiving with no online access or management tools. Forwarded mail and package storage typically comes at an additional cost.
  • Limited Security: Standard PO Boxes are simply locked boxes. They may not offer the same level of security and protection from theft compared to a private mailbox service provider.
  • Limited Location Options: USPS PO Boxes are only available at post office locations, which may not be convenient for everyone, especially those with busy schedules or limited mobility.

2. iPostal1 Mailbox Rentals:

  • Value Pricing: Starting at just $9.99 per month, iPostal1 mailbox rentals include a real street address, secure receiving of your mail and packages at a professionally-staffed USPS approved Commercial Mail Receiving Agency (CMRA), and 24/7 digital access to your mail.
  • Online Mail Management: Manage your mail from anywhere using our app or website, forward important documents and packages, shred unwanted items, and even deposit checks in your bank account – all from your computer or mobile device.
  • Greater Security: Your mail and packages are received and securely stored until you request that they be forwarded, scanned, shredded, discarded, or picked up, protected from porch pirates and inclement weather.
  • Professional Business Image: Project a professional image with a real street address on your business cards, website, and marketing materials.
  • Nationwide Coverage: With a network of over 3,000 virtual mailbox locations worldwide, you're sure to find a convenient iPostal1 location near you.

3. UPS Access Point Lockers:

  • High Pricing: UPS mailbox rental costs vary by location and mailbox size, with small mailboxes ranging from $10 to $30 per month, medium mailboxes from $20 to $40, and large mailboxes from $30 to $50.
  • Package Delivery Focus: UPS Access Point Lockers are primarily designed for package pick-up and drop-off, not for receiving traditional mail.
  • Limited Mail Acceptance: UPS Access Point Lockers may not accept mail from all delivery carriers, which can be inconvenient if you expect mail from USPS or other carriers.
  • Inconsistent Locations: UPS Access Point Lockers are located within participating businesses, so availability and locations can vary greatly.
  • Inconvenient Sign-up: You cannot get a UPS Access Point Locker online; you must go to a physical location to rent a mailbox.

Mailbox Rental Frequently Asked Questions

What is mailbox rental?

Mailbox rental refers to the service of renting a private mailbox at a location outside your home or business. The mailbox rental location is usually a professional pack and ship store, prestigious office building or coworking space that you can rent to receive your mail and packages. Unlike traditional PO boxes provided by post offices, mailbox rentals usually come with a real street address and accept packages from any carrier.

What are the advantages of mailbox rental?

Advantages of mailbox rental include enhanced security and privacy, convenience, and professional appearance. Renters benefit from being able to receive packages from all major carriers, avoid porch pirates, and maintain a separate business address that can improve their business listings. Additional benefits include safeguarding personal mail from theft or inclement weather, reducing mail clutter at home, and dealing more effectively with incoming mail and packages.

What types of mailboxes can I rent?

Mailbox rental options include traditional PO boxes, virtual mailboxes, and digital mailboxes. Virtual mailboxes offer a physical address to receive mail and packages, enhancing privacy and supporting a professional appearance by keeping personal addresses separate. Digital mailboxes include all the benefits of virtual mailboxes with additional digital features, allowing remote management of mail and packages using a website or app. Managing your mail online with a digital mailbox allows actions like forwarding, scanning, or shredding, eliminating the need for physical retrieval of your mail and packages.

How do I rent an iPostal1 mailbox?

Renting a private mailbox with iPostal1 is quick, easy, and convenient. With just a few taps or clicks you:

  1. Choose from over 4,000 real street addresses where your mail will be received by trained professional staff.
