What is a Virtual Office?

A Virtual Office is a physical location that serves as your company mailing address and includes a local or toll-free phone number. In other words, it is a complete business presence. You can be working from home or anywhere, near or far.

If your Virtual Office location is used only as a business address, it is virtual address, but when phone and fax are integrated, it is a Virtual Office. Your phone service may be either phone forwarding with voicemail messaging or live call answering.

Businesses may set up one Virtual Office location or multiple locations in many different cities or countries to expand their company presence quickly, efficiently and economically.

Your mail and packages are received and stored at your Virtual Office space, to be forwarded, scanned or picked up. Customer-focused and progressive Virtual Office locations also include Digital Mailbox service, so that virtual clients can view and manage their mail from wherever they happen to be, whether from the web or with an app.

Virtual Offices are available in different settings, so you can choose the one that best meets your business needs and budget.

For example, a Virtual Office at a professional retail pack and ship store is the lowest cost form of a Virtual Office. Home-based businesses, start ups and entrepreneurs can afford it. It is a great choice when you need a company address along with local or toll-free phone and voicemail.

Alternatively, if you need a Virtual Office location with more panache or want a receptionist, directory listing or access to meeting rooms, and you have a higher budget, you might choose a coworking space, often located in a prestigious office building.

iPostal1 offers Virtual Offices at both retail pack and ship stores and at coworking spaces. Whichever you choose, the cost of our Virtual Office plans is only a fraction of the cost to occupy an office or rent a coworking desk on an everyday basis. An iPostal1 Virtual Office is the perfect solution to enhance your corporate image and grow your business.
