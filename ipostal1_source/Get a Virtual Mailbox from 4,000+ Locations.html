How do I arrange for my mail to go to my new Virtual Mailbox?

If you want only mail from specific senders to go to your new virtual mailbox address, then you need to give them your new virtual mailbox address information, including your person box number.

If you are using your virtual mailbox address for a temporary period and want to redirect all your mail, file a Temporary Change of Address form for mail forwarding. It is good for 6 months and can be renewed for another 6 months. Maximum use is for 12 months.

If you are no longer using your current personal or business address, then you can file a Permanent Change of Address form with your local post office, which will forward First Class mail free of charge and magazines for 60 days free of charge. Please note that, after filing a Permanent Change of Address form, the USPS will not redirect your mail to a second address, should you leave your first address. Your virtual mailbox address location will forward your mail to your new address for up to 6 months but will be required to pay for postage.
