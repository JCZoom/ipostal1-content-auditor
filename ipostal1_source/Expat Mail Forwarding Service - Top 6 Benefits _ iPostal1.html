How Expat Mail Service Works

  1. Choose a Location and Plan. Choose a physical address from  4,000 expat mailbox locations across the U.S. Then select the plan that is best for you, depending on how much mail you expect to receive.
  2. Redirect Your Mail. To enable your new location to receive mail on your behalf, the USPS requires a notarized Form 1583 along with proper IDs. Remember to file a Temporary Change of Address form with the USPS and/or notify senders directly to begin receiving mail.
  3. Receive Mail & Packages from Any Carrier. Because every iPostal1 expat mailbox address is a real street address, not a standard postal box, you can receive mail and packages not only from the USPS, but also from FedEx, UPS, and other carriers. We send an email alert and cellphone notification when you get new mail.
  4. View and Manage Your Mail from Anywhere in The World. Use our free app or any computer to view an image of the outside of each mail item you receive. With a click or tap, tell your mail center what you want to do with each item: forward to any address, open and scan mall content, discard/shred unwanted mail or schedule a pickup. You can even deposit a check by mail.
  5. Add Locations with Just a Click. Once you create your account, it is fast and easy to add additional expat mailbox addresses for business or personal use. Manage mail for all addresses from one login.

Expat Mail Service Frequently Asked Questions

What is expat mail?

Expat mail refers to postal services designed specifically for expatriates, or individuals living outside their home country, for managing mail and packages. Expatriates can receive, forward, view and manage mail with ease, regardless of their global location, using a virtual mailbox. International mail forwarding and USPS forwarding are also beneficial elements of expat mail.

With iPostal1, receiving expat mail is straightforward and stress-free. Virtual mailboxes allow you to manage your incoming mail online using our website or app, receive packages from all carriers like USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL and Amazon, and enjoy flexible mail forwarding to locations worldwide.

What is expat mail service?

Expat mail service gives individuals living abroad the ability to manage postal deliveries and forward mail internationally through a virtual mailbox. Important features of expat mail service include mail forwarding, digital scanning, and online management, allowing users to access their mail virtually from anywhere. This service caters specifically to the challenges faced by expats, offering flexible solutions for managing postal needs efficiently, from important documents to personal packages, with ease and convenience.

With iPostal1 expat mail service, managing your mail remotely is simple. You can access your virtual mailbox from anywhere 24/7 using our app or website, allowing you to view and manage your mail with just a few clicks. View a picture of the outside of every mail item, decide which items to forward, open & scan, recycle, or shred, ensuring you stay organized and connected.

Expat mail service allows you to stay connected with your postal mail no matter where you are living or traveling, with features that benefit expatriates and world travelers:
