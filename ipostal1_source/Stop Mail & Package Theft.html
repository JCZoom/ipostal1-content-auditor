  1. Choose a Location and Plan. Choose a physical address from 4,000 digital mailbox locations across the U.S. and abroad. Then select the plan that corresponds with how much mail you expect to receive, keeping your deliveries safe from porch piracy and package theft.
  2. Checkout. At checkout, provide your contact information plus credit card or PayPal account.
  3. File Form 1583. What is porch piracy?

Porch piracy refers to the theft of packages from a person's doorstep or porch. Thieves, often termed "porch pirates," target packages that are left on doorsteps by carriers like USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL and Amazon, seizing the opportunity to steal valuable goods. Various common remedies, such as security cameras, lock boxes, and neighbor surveillance, have been found expensive, inconvenient, or only partially effective.
